Solution of the depletion of the Ozone?

More than 20 countries had come together and banned not only the production of CFC’s but also any Ozone depleting substance, but it wasn’t easy. Tens of Thousands of jobs were lost, but thanks to their decision we are here today. They funded researches, gave a time period for such a drastic change to happen. The Montreal protocol for Ozone Depleting Substances emerged to preserve and rejuvenate the Ozone layer.
